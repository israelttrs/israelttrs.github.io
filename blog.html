Learn the Lingo

  • Mardi GrasThis is French for "Fat Tuesday", a reference to the fact that Mardi Gras is always a day before ash wednesday, the beginning of lent in Catholic faith.
  • CarnivalCarnival refers to the whole period of time leading up to Fat Tuesday.
  • Krewe Members who plan and carry out various themed parties.
